BBQ Ranch Chicken Tacos

Ingridients

  • Tortillas
  • Chicken
  • Corn
  • Black Beans
  • BBQ Sauce
  • Ranch Dressing
  • Optional:French onions

Instructions:

  1. Gather all ingridients
  2. In a bowl add cooked chicken, black beans, corn, BBQ sauce and ranch dressing
  3. Mix together well until everything is coated in sauce.
  4. Scoop the mix into tortillas
  5. Sprinkle french onions on top
  6. Enjoy
Note:

For easy serving, you can prepare the mix ahead of time & store in a tupperware. Layer on tortillas when you're ready to serve. To enjoy this on the go, use canned or packets of chicken, cans of black beans & corn and packets of ranch & BBQ sauce.
